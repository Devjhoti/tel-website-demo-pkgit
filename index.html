OUR CRAFT

Stories of Transformation

CHAPTER I

Built By Hand,
Backed By Purpose.

TEL Plastics began with a simple belief: that what's discarded doesn't have to be wasted. Every product we make starts its life as something someone else threw away — and ends it as furniture built to hold a family, or a container that holds a home together.

We test, inspect, and refine every piece by hand, because affordable should never mean disposable.

CHAPTER II

Closing The Loop,
Restoring Nature.

Our manufacturing process is completely circular. By collecting plastic bottles and household waste, sorting them with microscopic care, and shredding them into fine raw particles, we make sure they never enter our oceans.

Instead, they are reformed into durable, reliable items that look stunning and stand up to daily use.

CHAPTER III

Molded With Purpose,
Designed For Life.

From the plant-inspired sculptural vents of the Leaf Chair to household essentials, we design objects with ergonomics and aesthetics in mind.

We prove that sustainable design doesn't have to look raw or industrial. It can be sleek, tactile, and built to stand the test of time.

SUSTAINABILITY

Closing The Circle

OCEAN CONSERVANCY

Ocean Plastic Rescue

We intercept post-consumer waste along coastlines and riverbeds before it reaches marine ecosystems, transforming plastic pollution into high-grade household furniture.

CARBON NEUTRALITY

Co2 Reduction Impact

By recycling HDPE and PP polymers locally, we reduce the energy required for manufacturing by 75% compared to virgin plastic production pipelines.

CIRCULAR ECOSYSTEM

Infinite Lifecycle Loop

Every product is designed with mono-material structural polymers. When they reach the end of their decades-long lifespans, they are 100% recyclable and remoldable.

COMMUNITY IMPACT

Socio-Ecological Loop

Our sourcing hubs provide fair-wage employment for local waste-collector networks, offering clean-up incentives that rebuild neighborhoods from the ground up.
